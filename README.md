# 🛒 Retail Sales & Profit Analysis

> A real-world data analysis project completed as part of the **Tech Minds Practical Program**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Analyzed a retail business dataset containing **500 transactions** recorded between January 2025 and May 2026. The goal was to act as a data analyst — understanding the data, identifying patterns, and generating insights that support better business decisions.

**Tool Used:** Microsoft Excel  
**Skills Applied:** Pivot Tables, XLOOKUP, GETPIVOTDATA, Dashboard Design, Data Visualization, MIS Reporting

## 🔍 Dataset

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Total Records | 500 transactions |
| Time Period | January 2025 – May 2026 |
| Regions | North, South, East, West |
| Channels | Online, In-Store |
| Categories | Electronics, Home, Clothing, Accessories |
| Products | Headphones, Watch, Blender, Sneakers, T-shirt, Jeans, Backpack |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **North** leads in revenue (₹81,211) but **South** is more profitable (₹27,758)
- **Electronics** has the highest total profit but the **worst average margin (-23.82%)**
- **Home** is the most efficient category with the only positive margin **(+3.93%)**
- **T-shirt** is the only product with a positive profit margin **(+15.76%)**
- Heavy discounts of 15-20% **reduce profit per order by up to 79%**
- **Online** sells more units but **In-Store earns more profit**
- **June and December** show decent revenue but profit collapses due to seasonal discounting

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- ✅ 4 Interactive Charts (Region, Product Margin, Monthly Trend, Product Comparison)
- ✅ KPI Cards linked to live Pivot Table data via GETPIVOTDATA
- ✅ Region and Channel slicers — filter entire dashboard with one click
- ✅ Color coded insights — Green for profitable, Red for negative margins

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

1. **Replace heavy discounts with bundle offers** during seasonal sales — discounts destroy margin
2. **Prioritise T-shirt and Home category** — only consistently profitable product and category
3. **Investigate East region** — similar revenue to others but lowest profit by far
